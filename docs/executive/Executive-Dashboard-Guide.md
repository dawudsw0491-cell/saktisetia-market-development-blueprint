# Executive Scorecard

## Market Health Score

Mengukur kesehatan pasar secara keseluruhan.

Komponen:

- LHR
- TOC
- Coverage
- Territory Score

Interpretasi:

| Score | Meaning |
|---------|----------|
| 90-100 | Excellent |
| 80-89 | Good |
| 70-79 | Moderate |
| <70 | Improvement Needed |

---

## Opportunity Score

Mengukur peluang pertumbuhan pasar.

Komponen:

- NOO Opportunity
- Institutional Opportunity
- Community Opportunity

Interpretasi:

Semakin tinggi nilai, semakin besar potensi ekspansi.

---

## Risk Score

Mengukur tingkat risiko strategis.

Komponen:

- Governance Risk
- Coverage Risk
- Dependency Risk

Interpretasi:

Nilai tinggi memerlukan mitigasi prioritas.

---

## Growth Readiness

Mengukur kesiapan organisasi menjalankan roadmap pertumbuhan.

Komponen:

- Capability
- Governance
- Architecture
- Roadmap
